Celsius to Fahrenheit formula

Celsius (°C) to Fahrenheit (°F), how to convert & conversion table. How to convert Celsius to Fahrenheit The temperature T in degrees Fahrenheit (°F) is equal to the temperature T in degrees Celsius (°C) times 9/5 plus 32: T(°F) = T(°C) × 9/5 + 32 or T(°F) = T(°C) × 1.8 + 32 Example Convert 20 degrees Celsius to degrees Fahrenheit: T(°F) = 20°C × 9/5 + 32 = 68 °F>
